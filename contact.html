FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical project take? ▾
Most store builds and redesigns are completed in 5–10 business days. Larger projects with custom features or migrations may take 2–3 weeks. I'll give you a specific timeline during our initial discussion.
Do you work with clients outside my country? ▾
Yes, I work with clients globally. All communication is done via WhatsApp, email, and video calls. I'm available across multiple time zones and will always accommodate your schedule.
What do I need to provide to get started? ▾
For a new store: your logo, brand colors, product details, and any reference sites you like. For a redesign or migration: access to your current platform. I'll guide you through everything in our kickoff call.
Do you offer revisions? ▾
Yes, every project includes revision rounds so we can fine-tune until you're completely happy. My goal is a result you're proud to show off, not just a box checked.
Can I order through Fiverr? ▾
Absolutely. You can place an order directly through my Fiverr profile for secure payment and project management through the platform. Use the contact form above if you'd prefer a direct quote first.
